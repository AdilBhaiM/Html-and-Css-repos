Our Story

At Stable, we understand the unique challenges and opportunities that come with running a small business in the UAE.

Our founders, Tahem and Usman, are seasoned entrepreneurs with multiple successful exits under their belts. They started Stable out of a shared frustration with traditional accounting firms that were low-tech and lacked a deep understanding of how technology businesses operate.

We believe in the power of technology to transform and simplify complex processes.

Tahem Verma

Co-founder, Stable

Tahem is the cofounder of Stable. He also founded Mesha, accounting software for global companies. Previously, he cofounded and sold Enguru, India's most used language learning app.

Usman Sheikh

Co-founder, Stable

Usman is the cofounder of Stable. He also runs High Output Ventures (HOV), which is a venture studio that focuses on funding and building remote first companies shaping the future of work.

Our services are designed to take the burden of financial management off your shoulders, allowing you to focus on what you do best - innovating and growing your business.

That’s why we’ve built Stable to be different. We leveraged a team of experts to provide professional, efficient and tailored financial services for your small business.

Unlike many of our competitors, we're proud to be entirely self-funded.

This independence allows us to stay true to our mission and values, and to prioritize the needs of our clients above all else. We're committed to providing a service that is not only effective but also transparent, reliable, and personalized.

Ken Leaver
